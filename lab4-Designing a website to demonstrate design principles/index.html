Meet our Team

Keep your bike safely running by getting a professional tune-up today! When it comes to the safety of our customers, we take our responsibility in getting your bicycle tuned up and repaired very seriously. A well tuned bike is a safe bike.

Sarah

Our newest member to the team! Sarah and Emily have been riding together for a while and have always been close friends. Over the past years, Sarah has been a big supporter of She Repairs. We’re glad to have Sarah officially join the team this year as a Lead Mechanic.

Emily

Emily is a long time bike-gal and cycling enthusiast. When she’s not wrenching, she’s out riding, doing local races, touring, or kicking back and surfing her favorite cycling websites. A familiar face to anyone who cycles in the East end, down Frank Kenny Road, or along the Parkway, Emiliy is a proven professional.

Leila

Growing up, Leila was always fascinated by bicycles, trucks, and cars. Inheriting this passion for mechanics from her dad, Leila was from a very young age very happy to assemble mechanical components and perform repairs and so she decided to pursue a career in this field and was able to successfully complete her mechanics’ licence last year!
